Tribute to "The Mother"


"The architect of dreams, molding futures with tender hands and steadfast devotion. In her embrace, worries dissipate, replaced by a comforting sense of belonging. With each gentle touch, she imparts wisdom, nurturing not just bodies, but also spirits."Her sacrifices are silent symphonies, sung for the well-being of her children".

A mother's love knows no bounds; it transcends time and distance, encompassing joys and sorrows alike. In her eyes, one finds a reflection of unwavering support, a silent vow to stand by through every trial and triumph. Her laughter is a melody that echoes through the corridors of memory, a reminder of life's simple joys. Yet, in her quiet moments, she is a fortress of resilience, weathering storms with grace and resilience.

In her presence, we find solace, a sanctuary from life's storms. She is a teacher, a healer, and a friend, her love a guiding light that illuminates the darkest paths. A mother's essence is woven into the fabric of our being, an eternal bond that shapes us into who we are. In essence, a mother is love incarnate, a testament to the beauty of unconditional devotion".		 
                    Let's pledge to heal her wounded land,
                    With gentle touch and caring hand.
                    For she's our home, our sacred ground,
                    In her embrace, abundance is found.
                    
                    On Earth Day, let's stand as one,
                    To honor her, our task not done.
                    For Mother Earth, in her array,
                    Deserves our love, each passing day.
